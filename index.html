My Name is Dennis Alejandro Chirinos Quan.

I'm a young artist from Honduras, that has recently moved to Canada.
My specialty is interactive design and game development, I'm however,
versed in areas such as video editing, coding, programming,
background art, concept artist, designer, and image and magazine editor.
I'm a solo developer that is working on his own project which
already has a degree of success, and have worked on various university projects throughout 7 years.
I'm multitalented and can work on various fields with a degree of competency,
thanks to my university degree in New Media, which I'm currently completing.
In my work, I tend to embrace more attention catching visuals with bright
lighting that help bring focus to important details.
I'm a very polite person willing to learn new things, I try to be as friendly
and comforting as possible, and I enjoy being as productive as possible.

Artist Statement
My work is created not as a way to express myself directly, but rather to create products that directly appeal to me and my disabilities.
I tend to not be satisified with my work unless I personally enjoy it myself.
In a way, I don't expect my art to be enjoyed by many, but I hope to appeal to
those who enjoy the same things I do, and hope to find community from it.
As such, my work tends to be bright to accomodate my ADHD disabilities, tries to be as cheerful as possible to help others feel happy.
